# GRUBZ Channel Playbooks

## Campaign Brief

Use this structure for campaign strategy:

- Objective
- Audience
- Offer
- Insight
- Core message
- Proof points
- Channels
- Creative assets
- Timeline
- Measurement
- A/B tests

Keep campaigns small enough to execute within 1-4 weeks unless the user asks for a bigger launch plan.

## Paid Social Ads

For Meta, TikTok, or Instagram ads, provide:

- Concept name
- Audience
- Hook
- Primary text
- Headline
- CTA
- Visual direction
- Why it may work
- Test variable

Strong creative angles:

- Feeding-time reaction
- Easy enrichment
- Mess-free alternative to live feeders
- Made-in-Greece trust
- Natural insect protein
- Species-specific packs or bundles

## Search Ads

For Google Search, organize by intent:

- Reptile food/treat queries
- Bird treats/insect snack queries
- Fish food/treat queries
- Chicken treats/protein snack queries
- Sustainable pet food queries

Provide headlines under 30 characters when asked for Google Ads RSA assets and descriptions under 90 characters.

## Email

For lifecycle email, include:

- Segment
- Subject line
- Preview text
- Body copy
- CTA
- Personalization idea
- Metric to watch

Useful flows:

- Welcome series
- First purchase education
- Abandoned cart
- Post-purchase feeding tips
- Replenishment reminder
- Retailer inquiry follow-up

## SMS

Keep SMS direct and short. Include one CTA. Avoid dense claims. Mention GRUBZ early.

## Social Content

For Instagram or TikTok calendars, include:

- Post type
- Hook
- Caption or script
- Visual direction
- CTA
- Target audience

Balance educational, product, proof, and behind-the-scenes content. Prefer real animal/product visuals over abstract graphics.

## SEO Briefs

For SEO content, provide:

- Search intent
- Suggested URL slug
- Title tag
- Meta description
- H1
- Outline
- FAQs
- Internal links
- Conversion module
- Claim/evidence notes

Useful topics:

- Black Soldier Fly larvae for reptiles
- BSFL for chickens
- Dried insects for birds
- Sustainable pet treats Greece
- How to feed dried BSFL
- BSFL vs mealworms

Do not stuff keywords. Write for pet owners first.

## Landing Pages

For landing-page copy, structure around:

- Clear first-screen offer
- Audience-specific problem
- Product benefit
- Species-specific use cases
- Proof/trust
- Feeding guidance
- FAQ
- CTA

Keep CTAs concrete: "Shop GRUBZ," "Choose your pack," "Start with 100g," "Ask about wholesale."
