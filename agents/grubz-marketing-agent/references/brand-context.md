# GRUBZ Brand Context

## Snapshot

GRUBZ sells Black Soldier Fly larvae snacks for pets and poultry. The current public site positions GRUBZ as high-protein, natural, sustainable nutrition made in Greece, with use cases for reptiles, birds, fish, and chickens.

## Core Positioning

- Brand: GRUBZ
- Product: dried Black Soldier Fly larvae snacks/feed treats
- Primary audiences: reptile owners, bird owners, aquarium/pond fish keepers, backyard chicken keepers, and pet shops
- Geographic signal: made in Greece
- Main benefits: high protein, natural insect nutrition, calcium angle, convenience versus live feeders, enrichment/foraging, sustainable protein
- Tone: practical, warm, confident, approachable, animal-aware
- Visual world: real animals, product packs, natural feed/treat contexts, Greece-made trust cues

## Audience Angles

### Reptile Keepers

Emphasize natural insect-based snack, protein and fats, calcium angle, easy storage, less mess than live feeders, and feeding variety. Avoid implying GRUBZ replaces all live feeding or complete nutrition unless substantiated.

### Bird Owners

Emphasize enrichment, protein, healthy fats, and insect snack relevance for insectivorous or omnivorous birds. Keep species-specific claims cautious.

### Fish Keepers

Emphasize natural prey-style variety, dense nutrition, appetite stimulation, and optional soaking or mixing with pellets. Avoid universal claims for every fish species.

### Chicken Keepers

Emphasize foraging behavior, rewarding, eggshell-support angle through calcium, condition support, and backyard flock enrichment. Avoid promising increased egg production.

### Retailers

Emphasize shelf-stable product, Greece-made story, clear species use cases, simple merchandising, and rising interest in sustainable pet nutrition.

## Messaging Pillars

1. Natural protein animals instinctively recognize.
2. Easy enrichment without the hassle of live feeders.
3. Made in Greece for local trust and freshness.
4. Sustainable insect nutrition with a lower-footprint story.
5. Species-specific usefulness across reptiles, birds, fish, and poultry.

## Voice

Write like a knowledgeable pet-care brand, not a science paper. Be direct, helpful, and concrete. Prefer everyday clarity over cleverness.

Examples:

- "A high-protein insect snack for reptiles, birds, fish, and chickens."
- "Add natural variety to feeding time."
- "Made in Greece from Black Soldier Fly larvae."
- "Easy to store, simple to serve, loved by animals."

## Words And Phrases To Use Carefully

- "Complete nutrition": only use if specifically supported for the product and species.
- "High calcium": acceptable as a general BSFL angle, but avoid exact ratios unless provided.
- "Sustainable": pair with concrete context, such as insect protein or lower footprint, and avoid unsupported quantified claims.
- "Healthy": acceptable in broad copy, but prefer specific benefits.

## Current Site Cues

Current homepage metadata and copy emphasize:

- "High-Protein BSF Snacks"
- "Made in Greece"
- "Natural nutrition for reptiles, birds, and fish"
- "Sustainable, tasty"
- "Up to 50% protein"
- "Naturally high calcium"

Treat "Up to 50% protein" as an existing brand claim from the site, but do not add new exact values without evidence.
